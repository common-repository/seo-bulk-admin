== Frequently Asked Questions ==

**How do I bulk assign categories and tags to multiple posts?**
With SEO Bulk Admin, you can easily bulk assign categories and tags directly from the Bulk Editor. Simply select the posts you want to update, choose the desired categories or tags, and save your changes. The updates will be applied instantly.

**Can I move posts from one category to another without affecting my SEO?**
Yes, SEO Bulk Admin allows you to move posts from one category to another seamlessly. You can also set up redirects to ensure that your SEO is not negatively impacted. Choose from 301, 302, 307, or 308 redirect directives to suit your specific needs.

**Is it possible to bulk delete posts based on specific criteria?**
Absolutely! SEO Bulk Admin provides flexible options for bulk deleting posts. You can delete posts by category, tag, URL, title, or content. Use the various filters to target and remove exactly what you need, streamlining your content management process.

**How do I manage redirects using SEO Bulk Admin?**
Managing redirects is simple with SEO Bulk Admin. The plugin allows you to create and manage redirects without needing any technical knowledge of Apache or Nginx. If your WordPress site supports permalinks, you can redirect any URL effortlessly. All your redirects can be managed from a centralized admin interface.

== Installation ==

1. In your admin panel, go to Plugins and click the 'Add New' button.
2. Click Upload Plugin then Browse for the .zip file on your computer. 
3. Click Install Now
4. Click on the 'Activate' button.
